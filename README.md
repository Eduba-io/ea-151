# Greater Miami Chamber of Commerce · Landing Page

A bespoke, single-page partnership proposal prepared for the Technology / Innovation Committee of the Greater Miami Chamber of Commerce. Built to feel native to miamichamber.com: GMCC's deep green, civic gold accent, Chesna Grotesk / Puritan type pairing, and the measured voice of a 118-year-old institution.

The page sketches a Chamber-branded AI readiness cohort for member companies, carries the full pitch body from the fit analysis, and routes the reader to a 30-minute conversation with Matt Creamer (CRO, Eduba).

## Files

- `index.html` · single-page layout, no JavaScript, mobile-first responsive
- `styles.css` · brand tokens from `brand-pack.json`, 375px tested
- `logo.png` · official GMCC horizontal logo (copied from Brand Pack/assets)

## Intended URL path

`/for/greater-miami-chamber-of-commerce`

## Notes

- Eduba appears only in the footer ("Prepared by Eduba. eduba.io"). No Eduba branding at the top.
- All URLs render as real anchors with `target="_blank" rel="noopener"`.
- No pricing disclosed. Scope language only (cohort structure, program weeks, alumni network).
- KPMG UK framed as "one of the Big Four" per sprint disclosure rules.
- Ethics Engine and NLP Logix are not mentioned on this page; the Chamber pitch is methodology-led, not compliance-led or data-pipeline-led.
- Fonts loaded: Puritan (Google Fonts). Chesna Grotesk is the Chamber's first-choice heading font and is listed in the stack; browsers without the licensed face will fall back to Puritan, matching the Chamber's own site behavior.
